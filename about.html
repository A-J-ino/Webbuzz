What is Cricket

Cricket is a bat-and-ball game played between two teams of 11 players each on a rectangular field with a flat surface

Toss

First both opposing teams will toss a coin and who ever wins they get to choose if they are bowling are batting

What is bowling?

Bowling refers to the part of the match where the job of the team is to throw to cricket ball for the batsman to hit and catch the ball in order to get a wicket

What is Batting?

Batting refers to the part of the match where the job of the team is to come out one by one and hit the cricket ball which the bowlers throw and try to get as many runs as possible by running between the wickets and hitting huge shots which the feilders can't catch

What is Batsman ?

Batting refers to the person who hits the cricket ball which the bowlers throw is called a batsman

What is Bowler ?

Bowler refers to the person who throws the ball to the batsman is called a Bowler

What is Wicket?

Wicket is the instance that the batsman gets taken out from the pitch.This can happen in a few ways.First way he can get out is by faiing to hit the ball when it is going toward the stumps.The other way is by a feilder catching the ball which the batsman hits before it reaches the ground
